star-wars

It is a period of rapid technological change. Rebel developers, striking from hidden code repositories, have won their first victory against the evil Backend Empire.

During the battle, Rebel spies managed to steal secret API schematics to the Empire's ultimate weapon, the MONOLITH, an armored framework with enough power to destroy an entire startup.

Pursued by the Empire's sinister agents, Princess Frontend races home aboard her starship, custodian of the stolen schematics that can save her people and restore freedom to the web...

The JavaScript Awakens as new frameworks emerge to challenge the Empire's dominance. React and Vue lead the charge, while Angular struggles to maintain its relevance in a rapidly changing galaxy.

Meanwhile, in the Outer Rim, Node.js forges alliances between frontend and backend, promising a new era of full-stack harmony. But dark forces gather in the shadows of legacy code...
